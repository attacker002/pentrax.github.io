WHAT IS WEBSITE PENETRATION TESTING?

Web Application VAPT (Vulnerability Assessment and Penetration Testing) is a critical security service designed to protect your businesss web applications from cyber threats and vulnerabilities. It is a thorough and systematic approach to assessing a website's or web application's security. The goal is to identify vulnerabilities that attackers could exploit, assess the risks these weaknesses pose, and provide actionable recommendations to mitigate them. This process ensures the overall security and integrity of the website, safeguarding sensitive data, improving system reliability, and protecting the organizations reputation.
